What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are programmed to perform tasks that typically require human-like cognitive functions, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI is widely used in applications like autonomous vehicles, virtual assistants, and facial recognition systems.

What is Machine Learning (ML)?

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that focuses on algorithms and statistical models that allow computers to learn from data and improve over time. Unlike traditional programming, where a system follows specific instructions, ML systems can automatically learn patterns from large datasets and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.

Applications of AI & ML

  • Healthcare: AI and ML are used for diagnosing diseases, developing personalized treatment plans, and drug discovery.
  • Finance: ML models help detect fraudulent activities, make investment predictions, and automate trading.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: AI is critical in self-driving car technologies for object detection, navigation, and decision-making.
  • Natural Language Processing: AI powers chatbots, translation services, and voice assistants like Siri and Alexa.
  • Robotics: AI is used in robots for performing complex tasks in manufacturing, surgery, and other industries.
